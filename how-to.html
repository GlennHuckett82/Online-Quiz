How to Take the Quiz

  1. Click "Start Quiz" to load a random set of questions.
  2. For multiple-choice questions, select one answer then click Next.
  3. For True/False, choose either True or False.
  4. For fill-in-the-blank, type your answer then click Next.
  5. You can change an answer before submitting the final question.
  6. Submit the final question to view your score and feedback.
  7. Review explanations for any incorrect answers.
  8. Restart anytime to try a new random order.

Accessibility & Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Use Tab / Shift+Tab to move between interactive elements.
  • Press Enter or Space to activate buttons and choices.
  • Press 1-4 to quickly select an answer option (MCQ only).

Your progress and score are calculated client-side. No personal data is stored.
